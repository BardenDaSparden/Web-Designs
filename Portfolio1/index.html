About Me

I am a artist and software engineer from Austin, Texas. I was first introduced to personal computers when I was 6, with a NES emulator and a copy of the classic "The Legend of Zelda". My passion is excersising Creative Problem Solving via computation. I have eight years experience as a programmer, and a I have keen eye for graphics! I'm also familiar with networking, physics simulations, UX Design, and game engine architecture.

My specializations are Graphics, and UX Design. I also have a great deal of experience working with Game Engine Architectures.

I am an analytical Thinker who loves using Mathematics to solve problems using Computation.
